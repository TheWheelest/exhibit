History
It was a quiet raising, no fuss, no huss. Just a nice family in a nice village. Friendly people, friendly animals. Seldom issues, save for when the caravans came through with wares. Sometimes this village's women followed spicy and saucy adventurers. Sometimes those adventurers stayed behind and brought new blood do this small place. Aster grew up here and ended up leaving. On her own, because she wanted to do more, learn more instead of be a simple barmaid for the rest of her life. She does serve as one at random bars and taverns and inns as needed for monetary reasons. She often returns home for visits.
