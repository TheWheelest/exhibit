Vigilante

A vigilante-hero type, Big Cock appears as man with a rooster mask that that fights crime as a Hero. Although the name is somewhat humorous, his personality is somewhat dry and so is his humor, causing most of the comments to be ignored.

Even though he has a real name, every single time it was written down or taken, the papers were lost, or ink was smudged. It doesn't bother the hero in question though, he is more than happy to do his job without a name behind it as with any rightful hero.

Power

Big Cock's power is described as a single input, dual output ability. By increasing his own muscle density he can not only increase his own speed and strength, at the risk of being crushed by gravity if he ever overexerts this ability. This can also work for, objects as well, increasing the density of a weapon to make it hit harder.

Although, the opposite is also true. Big Cock is able to decrease the density of himself, granting him the ability to jump higher and float. Though, this comes with the risk of ripping himself apart as the lack of density allows his body to be torn apart by lesser forces.
