History

Haku's father made a pact with demons who devoured Haku's body while allowing Haku to gain demonic powers as side-effects of the curse.

Haku was abandoned by his father, later adopted and in sword fighting before getting prosthetics as well as his own name.

Once Haku was old enough, he headed out on his journey to kill all 12 demons and regain his body.
