Background
A DOG unit augmented by various cybernetic enhancements along with a neural link to his PACK.

DOG-04 is usually seen around high-value sites such as industrial factories or shipping yards as security detail.

There have also been sightings of other DOGS but nobody knows who let them out. DOG-04 is a male in his late twenties. First contracted after the performance of the upgraded DOG-03 proved satisfactory enough to warrant comissioning another with similar features.

Compared to DOG-01 this unit cannot match the brute strength that the earlier DOG unit was sold on. Instead, DOG-03 and DOG-04 units rely on cunning to compliment the earlier models and often serve as lightweight patrol.

The DOG-__ title is given to those who are currently employed by the PACK, and although the units have designations, the contractors underneath have been known to change over time.
