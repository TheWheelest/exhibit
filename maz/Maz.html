The fourth daughter of six girls, Mazabelle is the heir of a Sandanavian house in the year __62. Having spent most of her youth watching her father in the pursuits of politics. It was discovered that she had an aptitude for swift judgement and a strong stance on moral justice.

Thus, her father gave her the opportunity to follow after him, and she even learned to survive in the harsh tundras without aid. She became a familiar figure next to her father, wearing her family crest emblazoned on her coat and linked with a gold chain. Eventually it was decided that she would be the one to take the mantle after her father having proven her distinction and the lack of a son.

Tradition dictates men lead the house, she took the name Leonir after her father retired, becoming the first woman in centuries to lead in her home country. Though conflict stirs, as some of her siblings turn to modern ways of governance in senates rather than the houses of old.
